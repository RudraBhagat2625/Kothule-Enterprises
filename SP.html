Shipping and Delivery

1. Delivery Locations

Pan India Delivery: We offer delivery services to all locations across India. No matter where you are, we can deliver our products to your doorstep.

2. Delivery Timeframes

Standard Products: Delivery times for standard products vary based on the location and size of the order. Typically, standard products are delivered within 3-7 business days.
Custom Orders: Custom orders, including personalized signage, trophies, and engraving, may have different delivery times based on the complexity of the order. We strive to complete and deliver custom orders within 5-10 business days.
Rush Orders: For urgent requirements, we offer expedited delivery services. CNC cutting, UV printing, granite stone engraving, and marble stone engraving can be delivered within one day. LED letters, traffic signboards, mementos, custom trophies, and corporate gifts can be delivered within two days. Please contact us to confirm availability and additional charges for rush orders.

3. Shipping Costs

Standard Shipping: Shipping costs are calculated based on the size, weight, and destination of the order. Shipping charges will be provided at the time of order confirmation.
Free Shipping: We may offer free shipping promotions on certain products or orders. Please check our website or contact us for current offers.

4. Packaging

Secure Packaging: All orders are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. We use high-quality materials to protect your products during transit.

5. Tracking Your Order

Order Tracking: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this number to track the status of your delivery through our shipping partner's website.

6. Delivery Issues

Delayed Deliveries: While we strive to deliver all orders on time, there may be occasional delays due to unforeseen circumstances. If your order is delayed, we will notify you promptly and provide an updated delivery timeline.
Damaged or Lost Shipments: If your order arrives damaged or is lost during transit, please contact us immediately. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive a replacement or refund.

7. International Shipping

Currently Not Available: At this time, we only offer delivery services within India. We are working on expanding our delivery network to include international destinations in the future.
