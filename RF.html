Refund Policy

1. Overview

At Four Artisans, we strive to ensure our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. We offer a comprehensive refund and return policy to address any issues that may arise with your order.

2. Return Policy

Eligibility for Returns: Customized products are non-returnable unless there is a manufacturing defect or error on our part. Standard products may be eligible for return.
Time Frame: Requests for returns must be made within 7 days of delivery.
Condition: Products must be returned in their original condition, unused, and in their original packaging.

3. Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refunds: Refunds are issued for products that are defective or incorrect due to our error. Custom orders are not eligible for refunds unless there is a manufacturing defect.
Process: To initiate a refund, please contact us with your order details and a description of the issue. We may request photos or additional information to process your request.
Refund Method: Refunds will be processed using the original payment method. Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

4. Replacement Policy

Defective Products: If a product is found to be defective, we will offer a replacement at no additional cost. The defective product must be returned to us before the replacement is issued.
Incorrect Orders: If you receive an incorrect product, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for the correct product to be shipped and the incorrect product to be returned.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Customized Products: Custom-made products are non-returnable unless there is a manufacturing defect.
Used Products: Products that have been used or damaged after delivery are not eligible for return.
