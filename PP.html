Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect personal details such as your name, contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address), and payment details.
Business Information: Information related to your company, including company name, address, and project details.
Technical Information: Information about your device and usage of our website, including IP address, browser type, and browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Information

To Provide Services: To process orders, deliver products and services, and manage customer accounts.
To Communicate: To send updates, newsletters, and promotional materials, and to respond to inquiries and customer service requests.
To Improve Our Services: To analyze data and usage patterns to enhance our products, services, and website functionality.
For Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and the rights of our clients.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:
Service Providers: Trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as payment processors, delivery services, and marketing partners.
Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose your information to relevant authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and access controls.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information. Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations. Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and gather usage data. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through our website or direct communication.
